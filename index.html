An AAC for watching Soccer

Soccer games are often quite emotional. The game can break your heart, or lead you into giddy euphoria. Fans should all have a way to communicate the emotions they're feeling throughout the game. This AAC allows the user to express key positive and negative emotional reactions typical in a game. The user must enter their team, because teams have different songs for their players. Individual songs are not implemeted at this time. Also, different colors may be happy or sad to different teams. For example, a fan of Tottenham Hotspur will likely hate red because it is the color of their rival team, Arsenal.
A final prototype of this would also feature animations and would likely be hooked up to bigger buttons that are not mousepads. It should be satisfying to express euphoria, so there should be animations as well. Furthermore, it should be customizable in terms of your own personal appearance. Most of the pictures I used were men, and people might not feel truly represented by this.

  • Cheering Fans cheer when they are happy, often when their team does something good.
  • Whistling Fans whistle when the opposing team has the ball. This usually comes from anger, excitement, and a desire to intimidate the other team.
  • Singing Singing conveys support to the team or individuals, and often comes with pride, happiness, and passion.
  • Groaning Fans often groan when their team does something bad.This conveys disappointment and unhappiness.
  • Total Euphoria The highest hights, giddiness, euphoria, disbelief and joy. Reserved for last-minute winners (for example, see Tottenham Hotspur's recent last-minute victory over Ajax.
  • Total Despair The lowest lows. Desolation, disappointment, disbelief, anger, sadness. The opposite of Total Euphoria.
